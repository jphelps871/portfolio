About me.

I got into web development when I was 14, of course I did not quite understand what web development was, or what I was even doing. I created obscure websites with my dad, mostly about space and our thoughts on the future of space.

However, as things go I stopped and completely forgot the fun I had. Fast forward to my Masters, where we were given the opportunity to learn Python and my passion for web development took off. Since then and into the days of Covid, I have not stopped creating, designing and developing all things related to coding and web development.

From learning on Codecademy and Treehouse to creating websites for clients, tacking questions on Codewars and developing immersed projects. All of which has led me to become a thoughtful, unique programmer with a passion in front-end web development.
